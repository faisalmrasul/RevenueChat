# AI Chatbot Platform

An intelligent AI assistant platform that rewards contributors for helping improve the system.

## Overview

This platform connects users with an AI assistant while creating opportunities for experts to contribute their knowledge and earn compensation for their valuable input.

## For Users

Chat with an intelligent AI assistant to:
- Get help and support
- Find information
- Have meaningful conversations

Multiple plans available based on your usage needs.

## For Contributors

Share your expertise and get rewarded. Contributors can help improve the AI through:
- Training and feedback
- Industry-specific knowledge sharing
- Data contributions
- Content quality review

### Contributor Benefits

- **Revenue Sharing**: Earn passive income when users interact with AI improvements derived from your contributions
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Track your earnings and contribution impact live
- **Monthly Payouts**: Automatic payments based on contribution value
- **Full Transparency**: Clear visibility into earnings and performance metrics
- **Scalable Income**: Your earnings grow as the platform expands
